FAQs

Quick answers to common questions about RecoMagic.

How are projects recommended to me?
Recommendations are based on your profile inputs such as field, target role, skill level, tech preference, and interest tags. The better your profile details, the better the match quality.
What is the difference between Explorer and Premium?
Explorer gives limited access and smaller project visibility, while Premium unlocks broader project access, more saved progress, and deeper guided support features.
Can I continue projects later?
Yes. Your project progress is tracked so you can return anytime and continue from your current phase and completed tasks.
How should I fill my profile for better results?
Use specific entries instead of generic ones. For example, write "Data Science" for field, "Data Analyst" for role, and concrete tools like "Python, SQL, Power BI" in tech preferences.
